Siphons
2020


Siphons are essential part of plumbing. They are use to evacuate wastewater and to avoid the back up of bad smell.
Make with PVC, they can be recycle by mecanic or chimic process but they are poorly developed and expensive.
Finally, the last process is energy recovery by incineration.
I decide to revalue siphons, keeping their shape and assembly them and create a lamp and a vase, in order to avoid polluting transformation
