Eclipse
2020


Projet d'atelier sous la direction de Julie Meunier


Shape research about textile tension. Each research use the same process : a tissue (here jersey polyester) is put on a cardboard tube and the tissue is drawn down by a string.

After this research and with the theme of shelter, I decide to create a sun umbrella. The orientation is inverse, the tissue is now drawn up. The typical shape of sun umbrella is recreate with textile tension and this avoids the use of many articulated arms and refine this object
This sun umbrella has a 2m50 mast in aluminium with a hoop on it. This hoop is covered by a tissue in cotton and polyester drawn by a rope passing throught the top of the mast. The rope is hold by a deat.
